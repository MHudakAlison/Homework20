Department of Homeland Security Redesign Case Study

Research

Based on our research my team and I found the website to be poorly designed, inadequate to users needs, lacking a clear user path, and overwhelmed with information that was not pertinent.

Iterations

My team and I developed a few iterations based on testing and feedback from users regarding what type of layout and flow would be easy for users to navigate and help garner interest from potential applicants.
